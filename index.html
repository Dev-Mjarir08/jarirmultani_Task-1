Our Story

The Grandview Journey

From a single office to a nationwide legacy — every milestone is a chapter we're proud of.

2009

Company Founded

Grandview Estates opened its doors in Mumbai with a team of 5, a bold vision, and a single mandate: redefine luxury.

2012

First Landmark Project

Delivered Azure Residences — a 48-unit boutique tower in Bandra that sold out in 6 days, setting a new benchmark.

2015

100+ Properties Sold

Crossed the 100-property milestone and won the National Realty Excellence Award for two consecutive years.

2018

Expanded to 8 Cities

Launched regional offices in Bengaluru, Hyderabad, Pune, Chennai, Delhi, Kolkata, Ahmedabad, and Chandigarh.

2022

1,000+ Happy Clients

Celebrated over a thousand families who trusted us to find their forever home or their best investment.

2025+

Global Expansion

Partnering with international brokerages in Dubai and Singapore — bringing world-class properties to Indian investors.
